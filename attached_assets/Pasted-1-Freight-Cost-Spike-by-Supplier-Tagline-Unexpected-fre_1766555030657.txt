1. Freight Cost Spike by Supplier

Tagline
Unexpected freight cost increase for a supplier

Technical Definition

Metric: FreightChargesUSD / PurchPriceUSD

Grain: VendName + month(PurchDate)

Baseline: trailing 6-month rolling average per supplier

Trigger: current month ratio > baseline × 1.25

Output: supplier, delta %, affected ItemIds
